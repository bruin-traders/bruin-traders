Bruin Traders

Bruin Traders is a group of students from diverse backgrounds who all share a strong
interest in the field of trading and quantitative finance.

With the growing interest in trading and quantitative finance, we hope to provide students
with learning and networking opportunities through info sessions and a workshop series, with more
possible events in the future.

Quantitative Finance Fall Workshop

This Fall, we plan to host a workshop/accelerator program featuring classes, exclusive networking sessions, and guest speakers from industry. Stay Tuned...

What Is Quantitative Finance Event

Interested in learning about quantitative finance? Come to the “What is Quantitative Finance” event on Wednesday, May 24 at 7-8pm @ Bunche 1209B. Followed by a Q&A with a panel of people working at Citadel, IMC, and Belvedere.

Bringing Quantitative Finance to UCLA

We will be hosting info sessions, poker nights, and small competitions open to everyone at UCLA next school year. General member applications will open up at that time.

Quantitative Finance is an exciting and lucrative field with growing. Bruin Traders seeks to expand this community at UCLA and help more students break into the industry
